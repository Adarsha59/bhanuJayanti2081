Welcome to Bhanu Jayanti 2081

Join us in celebrating the life and works of Bhanu Bhakta Acharya, the first poet of Nepal, known for translating the Ramayana into Nepali.

About the Event

Bhanu Jayanti is celebrated every year to honor the contributions of Bhanu Bhakta Acharya to Nepali literature. The event features cultural programs, poetry recitations, and discussions on his works.

Event Schedule

  • 10:00 AM - Opening Ceremony
  • 11:00 AM - Poetry Recitation
  • 1:00 PM - Lunch Break
  • 2:00 PM - Cultural Programs
  • 4:00 PM - Discussion on Bhanu Bhakta's Works
  • 6:00 PM - Closing Ceremony
